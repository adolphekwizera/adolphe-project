Our Achievement

We are a technology company first, but with a major focus on consumer well being through a heathy diet. Most peaple are very busy with their jobs, family and friends, and other important activities, which doesn't leave much time for cooking. This might lead to a poor diet and lasting health consequences. We want to solve this problem by using an AI-Centric approach. Users can use our app to select their diet and foods they like and dislike, and our AI algorithm will create a custom and individual weekly meel plan. But we don't stop there. we partner with restaurent and other cooking partner to actually cook and deliver all meals from the generated meals plan, in selected cities. All this will be packed up in monthly subscription, where user can choose betwwenreceiving one or two meals per day, every single day of the month.
